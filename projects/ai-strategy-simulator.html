Project Brief: AI Strategy Simulator

Scenario inputs with a simple scoring model to prioritize AI initiatives.

Scenario inputs

Inputs included business value, feasibility, data readiness, and risk. We assigned weights to reflect strategic priorities and recalibrated with stakeholders.

Simple scoring model

The model applied normalized scores with weighted sums to produce an overall priority score. The intent was speed and clarity—not perfection.

Insights and prioritization

  • Clarified top 3 initiatives for pilot resourcing.
  • Surfaced data-readiness gaps to unblock next steps.

Metrics

  • Reduced time-to-insight for early-stage strategy sessions.
  • Improved cross-functional alignment with transparent criteria.

Role

Defined criteria with stakeholders, implemented a lightweight model in JavaScript, facilitated prioritization workshops.

Limitations

Heuristic weights and limited datasets; designed to inform—not replace—judgment. Future work: sensitivity analysis and richer scenario data.
