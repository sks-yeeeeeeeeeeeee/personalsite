Project Brief: Case Analytics Ops Dashboard

Ops dashboard linking case flow, bottlenecks, and KPIs.

Problem framing

Stakeholders lacked a consolidated view of case lifecycle metrics. Decisions were slowed by scattered spreadsheets and late KPIs.

Approach

  • Built a small pipeline to ingest CSV/exports; cleaned and joined in SQL/Python.
  • Selected KPIs focused on leading indicators rather than lagging outcomes.
  • Delivered pragmatic visuals (status, throughput, aging) for weekly reviews.

Outcomes

  • Improved decision cycle speed by ~12–18% (faster standups and handoffs).
  • Better surfacing of bottlenecks with clear ownership and next steps.

Role

Analyst → strategist: translated needs, defined KPIs, implemented pipeline, iterated with stakeholders.

Next steps

  • Automate refresh schedule and add SLA alerts.
  • Extend drilldowns to root-cause patterns (time-of-week, owner load).
